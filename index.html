About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Drexel University, working under the guidance of Dr. Ali Shokoufandeh. My research lies at the intersection of deep learning theory and practical applications.

My work focuses on developing novel architectures that incorporate mathematical principles from operator theory, spectral analysis, and dynamical systems into neural network design. I'm particularly interested in creating models that are not only powerful but also interpretable and theoretically grounded.

Beyond research, I bring hands-on experience from internships and collaborative projects spanning healthcare AI, cryptocurrency analysis, and social media mining.
